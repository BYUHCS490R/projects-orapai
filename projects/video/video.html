What are the positives and negatives of hosting your videos on YouTube?

Positives:Hosting videos on YouTube is free, fast, and easy to share. It also automatically compresses and streams your videos so they play smoothly on almost any device.Negatives: YouTube may show ads, distractions, or recommended videos you dont control. You also lose some privacy because your video is stored on a public platform unless you manually restrict access.

What is the most useful thing you can do with a transform?

With a transform (in CSS or graphics programming) you can move, rotate, scale, or skew an element without changing the rest of the layout. Transforms let you create animations, smooth movements, and visual effects in a clean and efficient way.
