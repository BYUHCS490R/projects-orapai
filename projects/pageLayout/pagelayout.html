BYUH

Brigham Young University - Hawaii is located in Laie, Hawaii, United States. It is a private college owned and operated by the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. Many of the students here come from different backgrounds from the world over.

This school has a mission to prepare students of Oceania and the Asian Rim to be lifelong disciplies of Jesus Christ and leaders. If you come to BYU-H to study, you will not just learn secular things but also spiritual with work-study opportunity to help you be prepared for leadership when you go back to your home country. BYU-H is a great place to study and grow, plus you get to meet people from different places in the world.
